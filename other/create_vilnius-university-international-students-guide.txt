
# Research & Documentation Task: Vilnius Tech International Students

## Objective

Visit the **official Vilnius University of Technology (VilniusTech) international students website**:
🔗 [https://vilniustech.lt/en/international-students/full-time-students/](https://vilniustech.lt/en/international-students/full-time-students/)

Explore **all related linked pages** and gather every piece of information relevant to **full-time international students**.

---

## Task

Using the collected material, prepare a **comprehensive summary document** organized so that each main topic appears as a **first-level chapter**, making it easy for students to navigate.

---

## Required Chapters

The document must include, but should not be limited to, the following chapters:

1. **How to Apply**
2. **Admission Requirements**
3. **Tuition Fees**
4. **Scholarships and Financial Aid**
5. **Legalization and Document Requirements**
6. **Study Programs** *(Bachelor, Master, PhD)*
7. **Additional Relevant Procedures and Information**

---

## Instructions

- Each chapter must contain **all details** found on the site and its related links.
- Do not omit any relevant information discovered during research.
- Structure the document for **clarity and easy navigation**.
