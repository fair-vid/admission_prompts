# 1) How to Apply

## 1.1 Where to apply (online application system)

*   Full‑time international applicants apply via the university’s online application platform (DreamApply): **apply.vilniustech.lt**. [\[apply.vilniustech.lt\]](https://apply.vilniustech.lt/), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
*   The application procedure described by VILNIUS TECH is built around this system: completing the form, paying the application fee, document review/recognition, interview, and conditional acceptance. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20252026-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/qualification-recognition/93866)

## 1.2 Application steps (end‑to‑end procedure)

VILNIUS TECH describes the following sequence for full‑time admission: [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)

1.  **Complete the application form** in the online application system. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
2.  **Pay the 100 EUR non‑refundable application fee** using the invoice from the application system (must be paid by the invoice deadline, and no later than the application deadline). [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/tuition-and-other-fees/9603)
3.  **Document review & qualification recognition:** The International Studies Centre reviews your education documents and may consult Lithuania’s ENIC‑NARIC centre; additional documents/confirmations may be requested. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20252026-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/qualification-recognition/93866)
4.  **Interview step:** You are invited (via the application system) to record an interview; you must complete it within **5 days** at a convenient time; the faculty reviews the recording. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
5.  **Conditional Acceptance letter:** If successful, VILNIUS TECH sends an official **Conditional Acceptance** letter confirming admission to your chosen programme. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
6.  **Pay first‑year tuition + registration fee:** After receiving the conditional acceptance letter, you pay the tuition and registration fee for the first year. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/tuition-and-other-fees/9603)
7.  **Temporary Residence Permit (TRP) mediation workflow:** After fee payment, the International Studies Centre uploads the TRP documents to the EPIS system and sends you the **student mediation letter number**. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
8.  **Apply for TRP through VFS Global (non‑EU/EEA):** With the mediation letter number, you apply for TRP at a VFS Global centre; TRP processing can take **up to 3 months**; you collect the TRP card from the same VFS centre. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624)

# 5) Legalization and Document Requirements

## 5.1 What “legalization” means and when it applies

*   VILNIUS TECH explains that consular legalization certifies the authenticity of signatures/seals and is necessary for documents issued in one country to be accepted in another. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/legalization-of-educational-documents/9590)
*   Education documents must be either:
    *   **Apostilled** if issued in a country that acceded to the **1961 Hague Apostille Convention**, or
    *   **Legally legalized (consular/double legalization)** if issued in a country that did not accede to the convention. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/legalization-of-educational-documents/9590), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)

## 5.2 Legalization requirements listed by VILNIUS TECH

*   Documents must be translated into the destination country language **or English**; the translation must be fastened to the original and the fastening notarized; copies must be notarized/certified according to notarial procedures. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/legalization-of-educational-documents/9590)
*   VILNIUS TECH links to an official list of countries that have acceded to the Apostille Convention. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/legalization-of-educational-documents/9590)

## 5.3 Legalization in the TRP workflow (non‑EU/EEA)

*   Before applying for TRP, you must legalize **Police Clearance Certificate (PCC)** and educational documents; if your country is not approved for Apostille, you must perform **double legalization** (one in your country and one at Lithuania’s Ministry of Foreign Affairs). [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission-20262027-------scholarships/admission-requirements-for-international-students/legalization-of-educational-documents/9590)

# 7) Additional Relevant Procedures and Information

## 7.1 Immigration regulations (EU/EEA vs non‑EU/EEA)

### EU/EEA full‑time students

*   EU/EEA full‑time students must obtain a **Temporary Residence Certificate (TRC)** confirming the right of temporary residence in Lithuania. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624)
*   The TRC application is submitted through **MIGRIS**; within **4 months** of submission, the student must appear in person at the Migration Department to provide originals/biometrics and sign. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624)
*   After receiving the TRC, the student must **declare the place of residence within 7 working days** at the Municipality office (seniūnija). [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624)

### Non‑EU/EEA full‑time students

*   From **1 July 2023**, national visas **D** are no longer issued for full‑time students studying in registered higher education institutions in Lithuania; full‑time students must apply for a **Temporary Residence Permit (TRP)** instead. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/full-time/364088)
*   TRP can be applied for via **VFS Global** centres abroad (VILNIUS TECH points applicants to the list of centres and the Migration Department guidance). [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598)
*   VILNIUS TECH notes TRP processing can take up to **3 months** and is integrated into its admission workflow via a mediation letter number after tuition/registration payment. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/admission/application-procedure/9598), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/immigration-regulations/9624)

## 7.2 Accommodation (dormitories, prices, deposits, locations)

*   VILNIUS TECH states students can live in a **university dormitory**, private hostel, or rent an apartment in the city. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/-accommodation-/9617)
*   International student dormitory is located on campus (\~7–10 minutes walk to several main faculties/administration); other faculties have different city locations and VILNIUS TECH provides maps. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/-accommodation-/9617), [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/contacts/vilnius-tech-maps/194420)
*   Dormitory addresses and contacts (examples from the accommodation page): Dormitory No. 6 (Saulėtekio al. 39A), Dormitory No. 1 (Saulėtekio al. 25), Dormitory No. 2 (Grybo st. 39), with administrator names/emails/phones listed. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/-accommodation-/9617)
*   Monthly prices are listed by room type (e.g., single rooms, double rooms by dorm, renovated rooms), and a **600 EUR deposit** is required and repaid if no damage is caused. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/-accommodation-/9617)
*   VILNIUS TECH notes that due to high demand they may not be able to accommodate students in single rooms; typical layout is a block with shared facilities. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/-accommodation-/9617)

## 7.3 Mentor programme (student support & integration)

*   Each incoming international student can be assigned a **Mentor** (a student volunteer) to help with integration into university life and daily life in Lithuania. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/mentor-programme/53970)
*   Mentors typically contact incoming students about **one month prior** to arrival. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/mentor-programme/53970)
*   The page also explains how to become a mentor (registration + training) and provides intake windows (mid‑May–end June for Autumn; mid‑Oct–end Nov for Spring). [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/mentor-programme/53970)

## 7.4 Medical care & medical insurance

*   VILNIUS TECH provides a list of state and private facilities (including dental and ophthalmology) and cautions that English‑speaking staff may not be available in all specialties; emergency number is **112**. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/medical-care-/10297)
*   **Medical insurance is mandatory** for all international students and must be valid in Lithuania during the study period. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/medical-care-/10297)
    *   EU citizens: European Health Insurance Card / form E111 is required. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/medical-care-/10297)
    *   Non‑EU citizens: insurance from home country for the stay period or purchase special insurance for foreigners staying in Lithuania. [\[vilniustech.lt\]](https://vilniustech.lt/for-international-students/medical-care-/10297)
