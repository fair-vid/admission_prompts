# How to Apply

To apply for full-time studies at Vilnius Tech as an international student, follow these steps based on the information gathered from the university's official resources. The process is conducted online and requires careful preparation of documents and adherence to deadlines.

### Application Steps
1. **Choose a Program**: Review the available English-taught programs at Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD levels via the university's study programs pages. Ensure the program aligns with your qualifications and interests.
2. **Online Application**: Submit your application through the official portal at https://apply.vilniustech.lt/. Create an account, select your desired program, and upload required documents (scanned copies initially). For scholarship applications, select the "Scholarship" option during the process.
3. **Pay Application Fee**: A non-refundable fee of 100 EUR is required. Pay via bank transfer or credit card through the VILNIUS TECH payment site. Incomplete applications without payment are not considered.
4. **Submit Documents**: Upload legalized and translated academic documents, proof of English proficiency, passport copy, motivation letter (for scholarships), references, and any program-specific requirements (e.g., entrance exams for certain countries or Architecture program).
5. **Interview (if applicable)**: Qualified applicants, especially for scholarships, may be invited to record an interview evaluated by the program director or faculty.
6. **Receive Decision**: After evaluation, you will receive an acceptance letter if successful. This is needed for visa and immigration processes.
7. **Pay Registration and Tuition Fees**: Upon acceptance, pay the 200 EUR registration fee along with the first tuition installment.
8. **Visa and Arrival**: Apply for a Temporary Residence Permit (TRP) or visa using the acceptance letter. Bring original legalized documents for verification upon arrival.

### Deadlines
- General Application: June 15 for non-EU applicants; August 1 for EU applicants.
- Scholarship Applications: April 20, 2026, for tuition-free or partial scholarships (Bachelor's and Master's).
- PhD Applications: Contact the Department of Doctoral Studies for specific timelines, as they vary.

### Additional Notes
- Applications for the 2026/2027 academic year are open for undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral programs in English.
- Ensure compliance with country-specific requirements and document legalization before submission.
- For PhD, applications involve demonstrating research achievements and obtaining recommendations from academics.

# Admission Requirements

Admission to Vilnius Tech for full-time international students requires meeting academic, language, and document criteria, varying by level (Bachelor's, Master's, PhD). All applicants must comply with Lithuanian immigration regulations and hold a passport valid for at least one year from entry into Lithuania.

### Bachelor's Degree Programs
- **Eligibility**: High school graduation certificate with a minimum CGPA of 60%. Check country-specific requirements.
- **Entrance Score**: Calculated from grades in Mathematics (weighted 0.4), Physics or another science/social science subject (weighted 0.4), and English (weighted 0.2). Grades are normalized to the maximum possible.
- **English Proficiency**: For non-native speakers (unless previous studies in English): Minimum IELTS 5.5 (no band <5.5), TOEFL iBT 70, or equivalents (e.g., PTE 48, Duolingo 95).
- **Entrance Exams**: Required for applicants from Pakistan, Bangladesh, or Nepal (OMPT Mathematics exam, minimum 60% for university Bachelor's or 50% for professional). Architecture requires an entrance test.
- **Documents**: Legalized English translations of transcripts; no unofficial or self-translated copies accepted.
- **Other**: Country-specific requirements apply; study gap not exceeding three years preferred.

### Master's Degree Programs
- **Eligibility**: Bachelor's degree in a relevant field with minimum CGPA 60%.
- **English Proficiency**: Same as Bachelor's.
- **Faculty Evaluation**: Acceptance from Dean's Office, including a recorded interview.
- **Study Gap**: Not exceeding three years unless justified by work experience.
- **Documents**: Legalized English translations of transcripts and degree.
- **Other**: Country-specific requirements apply.

### PhD Programs
- **Eligibility**: Master's degree or equivalent, with strong research achievements and academic recommendations.
- **English Proficiency**: Not explicitly specified; B2 level assumed based on general requirements.
- **Documents**: Official transcripts, research details, recommendations.
- **Other**: Contact Department of Doctoral Studies for details.

# Scholarships and Financial Aid

Vilnius Tech offers scholarships for international full-time students based on academic merit, extracurriculars, and nationality. Scholarships cover tuition partially or fully, with monthly stipends for state scholarships.

### University Scholarships (Bachelor's and Master's)
- **Tuition-Free (100%)**: For top applicants (min 80% grades/GPA); 4 years duration; requires no academic debts.
- **Partial (50% or 25%)**: For qualified applicants (min 70% grades/GPA); same duration.
- **Eligibility**: Academic achievements, motivation letter (1000-4000 symbols), 2 references, English B2, extracurriculars. Extra point if graduated in last 3 years.
- **Application**: Via https://apply.vilniustech.lt/; deadline April 20, 2026. Interview for qualifiers.
- **Notes**: Incomplete applications rejected.

### State Scholarships for Master's
- **Eligibility**: Citizens of specified countries (e.g., Armenia, Azerbaijan, China, Ukraine; quotas apply); Bachelor's degree, B2 language, acceptance letter. No prior Master's or funding.
- **Amount**: 560 EUR/month + tuition grant (up to national standard).
- **Application**: Through Education Exchanges Support Foundation (master.scholarships@smpf.lt).
- **Deadline**: Not specified (call for 2025 applications).
- **Notes**: Not for joint degrees with home-country mobility.

### Other Aid
- Scholarships for achievements in academics, social activities, arts, sports.
- Ukrainian-specific: 40% tuition discount for Bachelor's.

# Legalization and Document Requirements

Documents must be legalized for authenticity. Vilnius Tech handles recognition of foreign qualifications.

### Legalization Procedure
- **Types**: Apostille (Hague Convention countries); single/double legalization (non-Hague, e.g., via embassies).
- **Education Documents**: Legalize in issuing country (Apostille or MFA). Bring originals/notarized copies to International Studies Centre.
- **Police Clearance (PCC/NCC)**: Obtain in home country, legalize (steps include university mediation, 30 EUR fee, submission to Lithuanian MFA). Timeline: Weeks/months.
- **Steps for PCC**: Apply for TRP, pay fee, complete home legalization, send original to university, collect via courier.
- **Notes**: Start early; failures delay studies. Lithuania uses Apostille for its documents.

### Recognition of Foreign Qualifications
- **Handled By**: International Studies Centre (appointed by Ministry).
- **Documents**: Scanned copies online; originals upon arrival. Translations if not English/Lithuanian.
- **Process**: Assessed for quality, profile, content, workload, outcomes. Part of admission.
- **Timelines**: During application; appeals within 14 days to Centre for Quality Assessment.
- **Outcomes**: Determines eligibility; appealable to Ministry committee (not for program-specific fails).

### General Document Notes
- All non-English documents need official translations bound to originals.
- Country-specific: See Admission Requirements chapter.

- **Notes**: Comply with Lithuanian Government rules; delays if documents incomplete.
